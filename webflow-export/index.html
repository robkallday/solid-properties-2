FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of houses do you buy?

We typically buy houses that need repair. If your house needs a lot of work, we are interested. Although we don't purchase many properties in excellent condition, we can still help you out.

How is your company different?

We are not a nationwide franchise with thousands of locations and employees. Our company is small and we specialize in southern California. Your calls and messages come to us and we work with you directly. Large companies with an 800 number usually use an out of state call center and pass you around. We are experienced San Diego investors and work with you every step of the way.

How fast can you purchase?

Typically we need 7-10 days to buy houses. We can close on whichever date works best for you.  Let us know your timeline and we can make it happen.

How much would you pay for my house?

Since we are a cash buyer, we buy houses at discount. Our cash price is usually about 10% less from what you would get traditionally. For a no obligation quote, fill out our form and we’ll get back to you with a fair offer.

Can you renovate and sell my property for me?

Yes we can! As long as you’re not in a hurry and can be out of the house during renovations. We put out the money to clean, fix and renovate. Once we sell the property, these costs are deducted from the sale price.

Are you a We Buy Houses company?

We are a real estate solutions company and not one solution fits all. We purchase properties for cash but can also sell your property traditionally.

How are you able to purchase and close so fast?

Since we are a cash buyer, the sale process is quick.  We don’t wait on banks to approve loans or wait for inspection periods and long escrows. This allows us to buy houses fast.

Do I need to clean or fix my house?

Nope, we purchase the property in it’s current As-Is condition.  No need to clean or fix anything.  Feel free to leave any trash and unwanted items.  Just take what you need and we take care of the rest. We buy houses in AS-IS condition.

My house is in great condition, can you still buy it?

We mostly purchase properties in poor condition or in need of repairs. If you need to sell immediately, we can still make you a cash offer. If you're not in a hurry and your house is in great condition, we can help sell traditionally.  

Wouldn’t my property sell for more with a realtor?

Your house can sell for more but you don’t walk away with the sale price. Real estate agent commissions are around 6% plus closing cost and any repair requests. You’ll also need to prep your home and make presentable for open houses.

Have I seen your TV commercials?

Nope, we are a small local San Diego company and don't have the budget for TV advertisements.

Have I seen your signs in my neighborhood?

Nope that was not us. There are lots of San Diego companies that put we buy houses signs in neighborhoods. There are too many polluting the neighborhoods so we decided not to advertise this way.
